Classroom E-Voting System

Welcome to the Online Voting Platform

This system allows teachers to manage candidates and students to cast votes securely with unique IDs. Please follow the guidelines below before proceeding.

Teachers Tab Students Tab

Guidelines for Teachers

  • Login using your credentials to access the teacher panel.
  • Add candidates with a unique Candidate ID.
  • You can delete or update candidates before voting begins.
  • Ensure all candidates are added before students start voting.

Guidelines for Students

  • Each student has a unique Student ID.
  • You must enter your ID to vote.
  • You can only vote once. After voting, your ID will be locked.
  • Your vote is confidential and will not be displayed.
  • After all votes are cast, results will be available to the teacher.
